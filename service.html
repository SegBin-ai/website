SegBin Lite

SegBin Lite is a smart dustbin plate that automatically sorts waste, revolutionizing waste management and promoting sustainability.

  • Easy Waste Sorting

    Throw your waste into SegBin Lite and it will automatically sort it into different compartments.

  • User-Friendly Design

    SegBin Lite features a user-friendly design that ensures convenient and hassle-free waste disposal.

  • Efficient Recycling

    With SegBin Lite, recycling becomes more efficient, reducing waste and promoting a greener environment.

SegBin X

SegBin X is an advanced waste management solution that includes an automatic waste separator, taking waste sorting to the next level.

  • Automated Waste Separation

    SegBin X automatically separates waste, eliminating the need for users to manually scan their waste.

  • Smart Technology Integration

    With smart technology integration, SegBin X optimizes waste management processes, making them more efficient and effective.

  • Promoting Sustainability

    By simplifying waste separation and recycling, SegBin X contributes to a more sustainable and eco-friendly future.
